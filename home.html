Town & Country Market, located at 85 N Country Rd, Miller Place, NY, has long been a beloved institution in the community. As one of the area's earlier establishments, it has earned a reputation for being a cornerstone of local life, serving both the everyday needs of the residents and providing a welcoming spot for delicious food and exceptional service. Known for its top-quality deli meats, Town & Country Market proudly serves Boar's Head products, ensuring customers receive only the finest cuts available. Whether you're stopping by for breakfast, grabbing lunch on the go, or ordering dinner, their menu offers a wide range of hearty sandwiches, soups, fresh salads, and more. In addition to the extensive deli options, the market also carries an array of grocery items, making it a convenient stop for all your everyday needs. The market has expanded its offerings over the years, providing catering services for all occasions, from family gatherings to special events. Free delivery and curbside pickup options make it even easier for busy customers to enjoy the market's high-quality food. With its long history in Miller Place, Town & Country Market remains a local favorite, continuing to serve its community with great pride and dedication. Whether you're a long-time regular or a newcomer, you'll always feel like part of the family at this timeless neighborhood gem.
